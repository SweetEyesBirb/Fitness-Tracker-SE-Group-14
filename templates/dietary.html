Nutritional Information

Mediterranean Diet, known for its emphasis on whole foods and heart-healthy benefits. The Keto Diet, focusing on high-fat, low-carb intake for weight loss through ketosis, and the Paleo Diet, centered around ancestral eating with an emphasis on lean meats and plant-based foods, can also be highlighted. Vegetarian and vegan diets, promoting plant-based eating, and intermittent fasting, involving cycling between eating and fasting periods, are other options. The DASH Diet, designed to lower blood pressure, and the flexible Flexitarian Diet, offering a balanced approach with occasional meat consumption.
